FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Steaksta ?
Steaksta is a website dedicated to sharing delicious cuisines, culinary tips, and food-related articles. We strive to inspire and empower home cooks and food enthusiasts to explore their passion for amazing dishes.

How can I navigate through Steaksta ?
Navigating through Steaksta is easy! You can use the menu bar at the top right corner of the homepage to browse different categories such as Cafés, Coffee, and Restaurants.

Can I submit my own experiences to Steaksta ?
Absolutely! We would love to feature your food critiques from our readers. As of right now we are still under website construction and soon you will be able to submit your experiences to our website through a “Submit Experience” page, where you’ll find a form to fill out with all the necessary details. We’ll review your submission, and if it meets our criteria, it will be published on our site.

Are the reviews on Steaksta valid?
The reviews on Steaksta are all of my personal experiences and opinions of each individual establishment. I am a food enthusiast and feel like I have a good understanding of what a fine establishment is in the culinary industry. I have visited tens of thousands of restaurants, diners, cafés, hole in the walls, street food, and bars.

Are the ingredients in the food easily accessible?
We strive to create a brief over view of dishes and maybe soon will focus on adding ingredients of those plates to our website. While some recipes may include unique or specialty items, we try to provide suitable alternatives or suggestions to make the recipes more accessible. If you ever have trouble finding a specific ingredient, feel free to reach out, and we’ll be happy to help.

Can I request a specific recipe for a dish or topic?
Absolutely! We value the feedback and requests from our readers. If you have a specific recipe or topic you would like us to cover, you can send your request through the “Contact” page or reach out to us via email. While we can’t guarantee that all requests will be fulfilled, we do our best to accommodate popular requests.

How often is Steaksta updated?
Steaksta is regularly updated with new content. We strive to publish fresh experiences, articles, and tips frequently to keep our readers engaged. However, the frequency of updates may vary depending on the availability of new content and the season.

Can I share the experiences from Steaksta on social media?
Yes, you are more than welcome to share our experiences on social media platforms! We appreciate your support and would love to see our website being enjoyed by more people. You can easily share Steaksta by using the social media buttons available on each recipe page.

How can I get in touch with Steaksta ?
You can reach us by visiting the “Contact” page on our website. The contact link will be at the bottom of each page under “Contact”. There, you’ll find a contact form to fill out with your inquiry or message. Alternatively, you can send us an email at Andy@steaksta.com . We strive to respond to all queries and feedback as soon as possible.

Are there any advertisements on Steaksta ?
As a means to support the maintenance and operation costs of our website, we may display relevant advertisements. However, as of right now there is no advertisements on Steaksta and when there will be we will make the most effort to ensure that the ads are non-intrusive and do not compromise the user experience on Steaksta.
